Our Ongoing Projects

At Tekeleza Hub, we are committed to making a difference through community-driven tech projects. Explore our current initiatives and get involved!

Tech for Education

Objective: Provide digital literacy training to underserved communities and equip students with the skills needed for the digital age.

Status: upcoming

Description: This project aims to create accessible tech hubs in schools and communities, where individuals can learn coding, digital marketing, and cybersecurity skills. We're working with local schools and NGOs to offer free workshops and resources.

Women in Tech Empowerment

Objective: Encourage more women to pursue careers in tech through mentorship, scholarships, and networking opportunities.

Status: Upcoming

Description: This initiative is designed to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry. We are partnering with organizations that offer mentorship programs and tech scholarships for women looking to enter or advance in the tech field.

Startup Incubator Program

Objective: Support and accelerate local tech startups through resources, mentoring, and networking.

Status: Upcoming

Description: We provide a platform for early-stage tech startups to scale by offering funding opportunities, mentoring, and access to a network of investors and entrepreneurs. Our incubator aims to foster innovation in the Kenyan tech ecosystem.
